Frequent questions

What does it cost?

Fixed package prices instead of open-ended hourly billing: you know the price before I start. The initial consultation gives you an honest assessment, free of charge.

Am I locked into a contract?

No. Projects are one-off fixed-price engagements. Monthly care can be cancelled. I'd rather convince through work than through contract terms.

What happens in an emergency or data loss?

That's exactly what tested backups are for: we verify restores before the emergency, and document how they work.

Will I understand what you're doing?

Yes, that's part of the job. No jargon, everything documented in writing and explained so you can pass it on to your team.

How do you handle data protection (GDPR)?

Essential for practices and law firms: a data processing agreement (DPA) is part of every engagement, data stays on systems in Germany, backups are encrypted, and I work aligned with the German BSI baseline-protection methodology. Data protection isn't an add-on, it's the default.

Do you use AI for development?

Yes, as a tool — the way a carpenter reaches for a machine instead of a hand plane. AI takes the routine off my plate and makes me faster. But the decisions — how something is built, what's secure, what fits your workflow — and the responsibility for them stay with me. Your data does not go into any AI; the tool helps me with code, not with your content. And AI is not a button that spits out finished software: demanding projects still take real time and care. Anyone who promises otherwise didn't build it themselves.
